Apply for Assistance

Thank you for considering Positive Tails in your pet’s time of need.

We hope we are able to be of assistance to you and your animal family. We have established the procedures listed below to help guide you as you decide if Positive Tails will work for you and your pet. Please review our procedures carefully.
Positive Tails is currently accepting applications for Veterinary Emergency and Referral Group (VERG) clients only. You must be recommended for the program by the staff at VERG in order to apply.

Follow the steps below to apply for assistance:
Download and complete an application form. Click for Application Form.

Complete and return the application (email, in-person, or fax – listed on application) along with the following documents FOR ALL ADULTS LIVING IN THE HOUSEHOLD (unless during an emergency situation wherein application decisions will be made without back-up documentation):

  • Copy of last year's tax return filed with the IRS
  • Proof of Unemployment, Social Security or Disability income, if applicable
  • 3 months of current bank statements
  • Veterinary Estimate

PLEASE NOTE:

If we review your documents and there are missing components, this will increase the time-frame of approving your application.

Any pictures or other documents submitted to Positive Tails assistance will not be returned. Please ensure that you have made copies of all documentation prior to sending.

You will not be considered for assistance if any of the following conditions apply:

  • You have already had services rendered prior to applying for assistance
  • You have applied for, and been approved for Care Credit
  • You have another option available to you to assist with payment such as family, friend, pet insurance, credit card, etc.
  • You are applying for assistance for routine care such as spay/neuter, vaccines, dental care, or routine checkups when there is another low-cost option available to you in the area
  • Signs of animal abuse that are the cause of the animal’s illness or injuries
  • Injuries/illnesses that are the result of gross negligence on the part of the client (i.e. walking your dog off lead and he/she gets into a fight causing injuries, etc.)
  • Injuries/illnesses that are the result of surgically altering your animal for unnecessary reasons (declawing of cats, clipping dog’s ears/tails, debarking, etc.)

Below are the requirements for funding approval:

  • Your pet is required to be spayed or neutered to be considered for a Positive Tails grant. You must agree to have this procedure completed at the time of the treatment you are requesting the grant for, or if your pet is not of age or stable enough, to come back within three months to complete this requirement at the expense of Positive Tails. Failure to do so will result in the transfer of the entire veterinary bill to your name, and you will be required to refund the funding supplied to you by Positive Tails and VERG of Brooklyn.
  • The animal must be a family owned pet with a good history of routine care with a general veterinarian.
  • The illness, injury or condition must be a treatable, yet urgent, condition with a good prognosis following treatment.
  • The family will be asked to contribute to the remainder of the balance of the treatment, however, VERG will work with each individual and/or family to assure limited financial burden is placed on the client

Once your completed application with supporting paperwork is received, it will be sent to our Granting Committee for review.

The Granting Committee will make the final decision on approval or denial of your application, and will decide the funding amount. Positive Tails typically has a response within 24 hours of submitting an application to our Granting Committee
